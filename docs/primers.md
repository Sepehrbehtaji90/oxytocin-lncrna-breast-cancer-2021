## Optimization / troubleshooting notes

- To reduce primer-dimers / non-specific bands, primer concentrations were reduced:
  - KCNJ2, CACNA2D1, CAMK2D, BALR2: reduced by half  
  - RGS2: reduced to a quarter  
- For lincZFP161 (LINC00667), cDNA input was reduced by half.  
- KCNJ2 showed a reported non-specific band at ~244 bp (expected product ~120 bp).
